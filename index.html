Lesson 1

Vocabulary
  1. wo: I, me      我
  2. ni: you      你
  3. ni hao: hello      你好
  4. ta: he, she      他
  5. shi: is, are      是
  6. shei: who      谁
  7. hao: good      好
  8. tang lao shi: Teacher Tom      唐老师
Dialogue
  1. English: Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni shi shei?
    Characters: 你是谁?
     
  2. English: Hello! I am Teacher Tom. Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni hao! Wo shi tang lao shi. Ni shi shei?
    Characters: 你好!我是唐老师。你是谁?
     
  3. English: Hello Teacher Tom, I am Felix
    Pin Yin: Tang lao shi ni hao，wo shi Felix.
    Characters: 唐老师你好，我是Felix.
     
  4. English: Hello Felix. Who is that?
    Pin Yin: Felix，ni hao. Ta shi shei?
    Characters: Felix，你好。他是谁?
     
  5. English: She is Carolina
    Pin Yin: Ta shì Carolina.
    Characters: 他是Carolina.
     
  6. English: Hello Carolina
    Pin Yin: Carolina ni hao.
    Characters: Carolina你好.
