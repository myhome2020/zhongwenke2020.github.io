Lesson 1

Vocabulary
  1. wo: I, me      我
  2. ni: you      你
  3. ni hao: hello      你好
  4. ta: he, she      他
  5. shi: is, are      是
  6. shei: who      谁
  7. hao: good      好
  8. tang lao shi: Teacher Tom      唐老师
Dialogue
  1. English: Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni shi shei?
    Characters: 你是谁?
     
  2. English: Hello! I am Teacher Tom. Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni hao! Wo shi tang lao shi. Ni shi shei?
    Characters: 你好!我是唐老师. 你是谁?
     
  3. English: Hello Teacher Tom, I am Felix
    Pin Yin: Tang lao shi ni hao，wo shi Felix.
    Characters: 唐老师你好, 我是Felix.
     
  4. English: Hello Felix. Who is that?
    Pin Yin: Felix, ni hao. Ta shi shei?
    Characters: Felix, 你好. 他是谁?
     
  5. English: She is Carolina
    Pin Yin: Ta shì Carolina.
    Characters: 他是Carolina.
     
  6. English: Hello Carolina
    Pin Yin: Carolina ni hao.
    Characters: Carolina你好.
     
Lesson 1 Review
  1. Who is Tang lao shi?

     
  2. Translate to Chinese: "Hello, I am Alex. Who are you?"

     
  3. What does lao shi mean?

     
  4. Which means "you"?
    A) wo
    B) ni
    C) ta

     
  5. Translate to English: "Ni shi Bailey. Ta shi Morgan."

Lesson 2

Vocabulary
  1. dong xi: stuff, thing      东西
  2. xin: new      新
  3. tong xue: classmate      同学
  4. lai: to come      来
  5. he: and      和
  6. bu zhi dao: do not know      不知道
  7. ma: put it at the end of a sentence to make the sentence a question      吗
  8. ye: also, too      也
Dialogue
  1. English: Ming Ming’s new classmate is Ben Ben.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming de xin tong xue shi Ben Ben
    Characters:明明的新同学是本本.
     
  2. English: Ming Ming’s new classmate has arrived for class.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming de xin tong xue lai shang ke.
    Characters:明明的新同学来上课.
     
  3. English: Ming Ming and Ben Ben take Ms. Ming’s Chinese class.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming he Ben Ben shang Ming lao shi de zhong wen ke.
    Characters: 明明和本本上明老师的中文课.
     
  4. English: Ms. Ming: Whose stuff is this?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi shei de dong xi?
    Characters: 明老师: 这是谁的东西?
     
  5. English: Ming Ming: It’s not mine. I don’t know whose stuff it is.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Bu shi wo de. Wo bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi.
    Characters: 明明: 不是我的。我不知道是谁的东西.
     
  6. English: Ms. Ming: Ben Ben, is this your stuff?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Ben Ben，zhe shi ni de dong xi ma?
    Characters: 明老师: 本本，这是你的东西吗?
     
  7. English: Ben Ben: It’s not mine. I also don’t know whose stuff it is.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Bu shi wo de，wo ye bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi.
    Characters: 本本: 不是我的，我也不知道是谁的东西.
     
  8. English: Ms. Ming: Is this Mr. Tom’s stuff?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi Tang lao shi de dong xi ma?
    Characters: 明老师: 这是唐老师的东西吗?
     
  9. English: Ben Ben: It’s not Mr. Tom’s either.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ye bu shi Tang lao shi de.
    Characters: 本本: 也不是唐老师的.
     
  10. English: Ms. Ming: Whose stuff is this?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi shei de?!
    Characters: 明老师: 这是谁的?!
     
Lesson 2 Review
  1. What does the opposite of "zhi dao" mean?

     
  2. Translate to Chinese: "This is not John's stuff."

     
  3. Define tong xue.

     
  4. What is the function of the word ma?

     
  5. Translate to English: "Wo ye bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi."

Lesson 3

Vocabulary
  1. yi: one      一
  2. er: two      二
  3. san: three      三
  4. zen me: how      怎么
  5. shu yi shu: to count      数一数
  6. ye: also      也
  7. kan: look, see      看
  8. lai: to go, to try      来
  9. dao: the eternal road of Daoism      道
  10. sheng: to sprout, to grow      生
  11. wan wu: all things      万物
  12. Shen me dong xi?: used when very confused      什么东西?
Grammar

Verb 一 Verb
Mostly used in speaking, infrequently used in written language.

Verb English Sentence Pin Yin Sentence Character Sentence
数一数 Do you know how to count? Ni zhi dao zen me shu yi shu ma? 你知道怎么数一数吗?
看一看 I’ll take a look. Wo qu kan yi kan. 我去看一看.
说一说 Please explain what you mean a bit. Qing jiang yi jiang ni de yi si. 请说一说你的意思.
听一听 I want to have a listen to this song. Wo yao ting yi ting zhe shou ge. 我要听一听这首歌.
试一试 You want to give it a try? Ni yao shi yi shi ma? 你要试一试吗?
Dialogue
  1. English: Ming Ming! Do you know how to count?
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming! Ni zhi dao zen me shu yi shu ma?
    Characters: 本本: 明明！你知道怎么数一数吗?
     
  2. English: Yes, I know! Do you also know (how to count)?
    Pin Yin: Wo zhi dao! Ni ye zhi dao ma?
    Characters: 明明: 我知道！你也知道吗?
     
  3. English: I know! Look! One, two, three!
    Pin Yin: Zhi dao! Ni kan! yī, er, san!
    Characters: 本本: 知!你看!一, 二, 三!
     
  4. English: Very good! I’ll go! The Dao sprouted one, one sprouted two, two sprouted three, three sprouted all things.
    Pin Yin: Hen hao! Wo lai! Dao sheng yi, yi sheng er, er sheng san, san sheng wan wu.
    Characters: 明明: 很好! 我来! 道生一, 一生二, 二生三, 三生万物.
     
  5. English: What?
    Pin Yin: Shen me dong xi?
    Characters: 本本: 什么东西?
     
  6. English: How am I supposed to know?
    Pin Yin: Wo zen me zhi dao?
    Characters: 明明: 我怎么知道?

  2. What word goes in between the verb.

     
  3. How do you say student in Chinese?

     
  4. What do you say when you are confused?

     
  5. How do you say all things?

Lesson 4

Vocabulary
  1. yi ge: a, one, an      一个
  2. ha: ha(sound of laughter)      哈
  3. ren: person      人
  4. yi si: meaning or definition      意思
  5. hai: still      还
  6. xiao: laughing, smiling      笑
  7. ming ming: clearly, obviously      明明
  8. yu zhou: the universe      宇宙
  9. lai: from, to come from      来
  10. hao: good, very      好
  11. gao xiao: funny      搞笑
  12. yi ge tong xue: a/one classmate      一个同学
  13. yi ge lao shi: a/one teacher      一个老师
  14. yi ge han zi: a/one Chinese character      一个汉字
  15. yi ge peng you: a/one friend      一个朋友
  16. yi ge dong xi: a/one thing      一个东西
Dialogue
  1. English: Ben Ben: Are you a thing?
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ni shi yi ge dong xi ma?
    Characters: 本本: 你是一个东西吗?
     
  2. English: Ming Ming: No! I’m not a thing, I’m a person!
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Bu shi! Wo bu shi yi ge dong xi ，wo shi yi ge ren!
    Characters: 明明: 不是! 我不是一个东西, 我是一个人!
     
  3. English: Ben Ben: Hahahaha, you’re not a thing! That’s hilarious.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ha ha ha ha, ni bu shi yi ge dong xi ！Hao gao xiao.
    Characters: 本本: 哈哈哈哈, 你不是一个东西! 好搞笑!
     
  4. English: Ming Ming: What’s funny? You’re also not a thing. You’re also a person.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Shen me gao xiao? Ni ye bu shi yi ge dong xi. Ni ye shi yi ge ren.
    Characters: 明明: 什么搞笑? 你也不是一个东西. 你也是一个人.
     
  5. Enlish: Ben Ben: Do you know what: “I am not a thing” means?
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ni zhi dao "wǒ bú shi yi gè dong xi" de yi si ma?
    Characters: 本本: 你知道: "我不是一个东西" 的意思吗?
     
  6. English: Ming Ming: You’re still laughing. I clearly don’t know.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Ni hai xiao. Wo ming ming bu zhi dao.
    Characters: 明明: 你还笑, 我明明不知道.
     
  7. English: “I am not a thing” means: “I am not from this Universe. I clearly am not a person.”
    Pin Yin: "Wo bu shi yi ge dong xi" De yi si shi: "Wo bu shi zhe ge yu zhou lai de. Wo ming ming bu shi yi ge ren."
    Characters: 本本: "我不是一个东西" 的意思是: "我不是这个宇宙来的. 我明明不是一个人."
     
  8. English: Hahahaha, that’s funny!
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Ha ha ha ha, hao gao xiao!
    Characters: 明明: 哈哈哈哈, 好搞笑!

  5. We live someplace very large with galaxies, planets, moons, and more. What do you call it in Chinese?
